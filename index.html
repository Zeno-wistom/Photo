Statement

Photography as a visual archive, interface as the frame.

This site collects personal photographic fragments into a quiet digital exhibition. The photographs remain the center; layout, motion, and computational color feedback are used only to shape atmosphere and reading rhythm.

It is designed as a small bridge between photography, visual design, and AI-era interaction thinking: restrained, responsive, and built for continued curation.
